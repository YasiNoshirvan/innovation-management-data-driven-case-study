# Data-Driven Innovation Management Case Study

This repository contains a course project developed for the **Innovation Management** course in the M.Sc. Data Science and Engineering program at **Politecnico di Torino**.

The project focuses on the analysis of **DBC Paper s.r.l.**, an Italian company operating in the paper and cardboard cutting and distribution sector. The aim was to study the company’s current business model, identify operational and strategic challenges, and propose a data-driven innovation solution to support decision-making and improve business performance.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Traditional industrial companies often rely on experience-based decision-making, manual workflows, and fragmented information systems.  
In this project, we explored how data-driven tools could help a traditional B2B company improve its operations, reduce inefficiencies, and strengthen its competitive positioning.

The proposed solution focuses on the integration of customer, inventory, production, and operational data into a decision-support system.

The main idea is to help the company move from a mainly manual and reactive approach toward a more predictive, integrated, and data-driven way of managing operations.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The project aims to:

- Analyze the company’s current business model and operational context
- Identify key inefficiencies and innovation opportunities
- Understand how data can support better managerial decisions
- Propose a data-driven decision-support system
- Improve demand forecasting and inventory planning
- Reduce waste and support sustainability goals
- Strengthen customer service through better information management

---

## 🧠 Main Areas of Analysis

The project includes several innovation management tools and business analysis frameworks:

- Company and industry analysis
- SWOT analysis
- Business Model Canvas
- Smart Strategy Board
- Value Stream Mapping
- AS-IS process analysis
- TO-BE data-driven solution proposal
- Expected business impact assessment

---

## 💡 Proposed Innovation Solution

The proposed solution is a conceptual data-driven system designed to support:

| Area | Proposed Improvement |
|---|---|
| Demand Forecasting | Predict future customer orders using historical and seasonal patterns |
| Inventory Management | Optimize stock levels and reduce paper waste |
| Production Planning | Improve workflow scheduling and reduce delays |
| Customer Management | Segment clients and anticipate customer needs |
| Sustainability | Monitor waste reduction and environmental performance |
| Decision Support | Provide dashboards and real-time operational insights |

The solution combines concepts such as predictive analytics, CRM/ERP integration, inventory optimization, dashboards, and sustainability-oriented decision-making.
