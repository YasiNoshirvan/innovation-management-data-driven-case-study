# Project Summary

## Data-Driven Innovation Management Case Study

This project was developed for the **Innovation Management** course in the M.Sc. Data Science and Engineering program at **Politecnico di Torino**.

The project focuses on the analysis of **DBC Paper s.r.l.**, an Italian company operating in the paper and cardboard cutting and distribution sector. The main objective was to study the company’s current business model, identify operational challenges and strategic opportunities, and propose a data-driven innovation solution.

---

## Project Objective

The goal of the project was to understand how data science and digital tools could support decision-making in a traditional industrial business.

The proposed solution aims to improve:

- Demand forecasting
- Inventory management
- Workflow scheduling
- Customer service
- Waste reduction
- Sustainability reporting
- Operational efficiency

---

## Methodology

The project followed an innovation management and data-driven decision-making approach.

The main steps included:

1. Understanding the company and its industry
2. Analyzing the current business model
3. Identifying operational problems and strategic opportunities
4. Representing the AS-IS situation
5. Proposing a TO-BE data-driven system
6. Evaluating the expected business value of the proposed solution

## Proposed Data-Driven System

The proposed system is designed to support managers through data-based decision-making.

The main modules include:

### Demand Forecasting

A forecasting module based on historical client orders and seasonal trends, with the objective of anticipating future demand.

### Inventory Optimization

A system for recommending the best paper rolls to use in order to reduce waste, optimize stock levels, and lower management costs.

### Client Segmentation

A module for grouping clients based on purchasing behavior, enabling more tailored services and proactive customer care.

### Workflow Scheduling

A planning module to align production schedules with forecasted demand and reduce lead times.

### Performance Dashboard

A real-time dashboard for visualizing key metrics related to production, warehouse status, customer orders, and sustainability performance.
