# Company Profile — DBC Paper s.r.l.

## Company Overview

**DBC Paper s.r.l.** is an Italian company operating in the paper and cardboard sector. The company specializes in the preparation, cutting, and distribution of paper and cardboard products.

Founded in 2004, the company later moved its headquarters to Candiolo, Italy. DBC Paper mainly serves customers in the printing, publishing, and light packaging industries.

---

## Business Activity

DBC Paper purchases and stores different types and sizes of paper and cardboard rolls. These materials are then cut into customized formats according to client requirements.

The company’s activity is based on:

- Purchasing paper and cardboard rolls
- Managing warehouse stock
- Cutting materials into customized formats
- Preparing orders based on client needs
- Delivering products with flexible timing and quantities

This makes DBC Paper an important intermediary between paper producers and downstream customers such as printers, publishers, and packaging companies.

---

## Customers

The company mainly serves:

- Small and medium-sized printing companies
- Publishers
- Light cardboard packaging businesses
- Customers requiring customized paper formats

Its value proposition is based on flexibility, customization, speed, and the ability to provide tailored paper and cardboard solutions.

## Core Values

The company’s strategy can be summarized around four main pillars:

### Search

Continuous search for high-value products, technological upgrades, and quality improvement.

### Service

Flexible customer service, customized formats, and availability of raw materials.

### Strategy

A diversified product portfolio and long-term relationships with suppliers and customers.

### Sustainability

Commitment to environmentally responsible practices, certified products, renewable energy, and waste reduction initiatives.

---

## Sustainability and Certifications

DBC Paper operates in an industry where sustainability is highly relevant due to the environmental impact of paper production, including deforestation, water consumption, chemical use, and CO₂ emissions.

The company holds important certifications related to sustainable forest management and product traceability, including:

- FSC — Forest Stewardship Council
- PEFC — Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification

These certifications support the company’s positioning in a market increasingly influenced by environmental and regulatory requirements.

---

## Why This Company Was Selected

DBC Paper was selected for this case study because it represents an interesting example of a traditional industrial business that can benefit from data-driven innovation.

The company operates in a sector where operational efficiency, stock management, waste reduction, customer responsiveness, and sustainability are strategically important.

At the same time, several areas could benefit from digital transformation and data-based decision-making, such as:

- Manual order processing
- Inventory management
- Forecasting customer demand
- Production planning
- Waste reduction
- Customer segmentation
- Real-time monitoring

For these reasons, DBC Paper provided a suitable case for applying innovation management concepts to a real business context.

---

## Main Challenge Identified

The main challenge identified in the project is the opportunity to improve decision-making through better use of data.

In the AS-IS situation, order processing and inventory management rely mainly on traditional and partially manual processes. The company uses basic stock tracking, but there is room for more advanced predictive analytics, real-time monitoring, and integrated digital tools.

---

## Proposed Direction

The proposed direction is the development of a data-driven decision-support system that connects customer data, warehouse information, production planning, and shipping.

The system would support:

- Forecasting future customer orders
- Optimizing inventory levels
- Reducing paper waste
- Improving production scheduling
- Enhancing customer care
- Supporting sustainability goals
